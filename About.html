Sapience
The company name “Sapience” came from the meaning of having the quality of being wise or having wisdom. The name Sapience not only matters about the members of the company, but it also corresponds to the game and the users. Sapience, the company that develops the “Smash news” game about determining whether the news is fake or not, needs to encompass the trait of wise men. Being wise and having good judgment not only for the members of the company but the users of the game Smash News too.

02

The brand of the company's product shall be renowned in its locality. Through the deployment of entertaining and enlightening applications, a world where critical thinkers are nurtured, reliable source of information is evaluated, and malinformation is minimized.
